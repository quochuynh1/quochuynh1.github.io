I'm a penultimate Electrical and Electronic Engineering (Honours) student 
at James Cook University, Cairns, with a strong foundation in analogue 
circuit design developed through university lab work and self-driven projects. 
I'm particularly drawn to the application of electronics in consumer devices and 
biomedical technology, where precision circuit design has real-world 
impact.

I'm currently seeking an internship role where I can contribute to 
and grow within a capable engineering team.

## Technical Skills

**Hardware & Design**
- Analogue circuit design — op-amp circuits, BJT/JFET amplifiers, 
  impedance matching
- PCB design and fabrication — KiCad, JLCPCB
- Embedded systems — Arduino, WeMos, Raspberry Pi Pico (C/C++)
- Test equipment — oscilloscope, function generator, multimeter

**Software & Tools**
- LTSpice (simulation), MATLAB (data analysis, curve fitting)
- Python (OOP, file I/O, Kivy GUI)
- KiCad, Arduino IDE

## Projects

A snapshot of my university projects — see the [Portfolio](/portfolio/) 
for full writeups:

- **5MHz Function Generator** — Colpitts oscillator with LC filtering, 
  adjustable amplitude control, and KiCad PCB fabricated via JLCPCB
- **Additive Audio Mixer** — Three-stage BJT/JFET design, simulated in 
  LTSpice, PCB fabricated and demonstrated live driving a 16Ω speaker
- **CNC Milling Machine Control System** — Modular C program on 
  Raspberry Pi Pico with 10-direction control and real-time emergency stop
- **NTC Thermistor Temperature Sensing System** — Full analogue-to-digital 
  pipeline with live ThingSpeak dashboard
